Oscar de la Renta Museum Poster

Challenge Statement

Graphic Design a poster using Illustrator or Photoshop for an artists' featured exhibit at the MET.


I created a poster announcing Oscar de la Renta's "new" exhibit at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. I chose a specific color scheme, created a vector background, and photoshops his previous works on the runway in the poster, adding depth and more dimension. Typography was also carefully chosen and placed.
